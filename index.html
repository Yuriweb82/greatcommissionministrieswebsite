aims

And objectives of the ministry:

(a) To propagate and disseminate the doctrine and ordinance of Jesus Christ for the betterment of the community.

(b) To preach the gospel of Jesus Christ, to bring together in congregation of Christian fellowship those persons who desire to support the missionary activities of this ministry.

(c) To train members of the community in evangelism work to further the objectives of the ministry and to improve the welfare of the community.

(d) To organise Crusades, Seminars, Conferences throughout the Republic of Uganda and outside Uganda aimed at the promotion of the objectives of the Ministry.

(e) Helping the Marginalised to eradicate poverty bring back happiness to the grieving
